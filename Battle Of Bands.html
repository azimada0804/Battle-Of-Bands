Generate Visuals

Fire up Google Gemini and generate your Band Poster. It MUST include your Band Name. Add your motto or campus name for extra flair.

EXAMPLE PROMPT 01

"Create a modern music band poster for a band called 'The Rock Rebels'. Energetic college music vibe, neon lights, stage performance atmosphere, vibrant colors, stylish typography, concert poster design, professional and eye-catching."

EXAMPLE PROMPT 02

"Create a futuristic music band poster for a band named 'Campus Chaos'. College students, energetic vibe, blue and purple neon lights, modern concert poster, bold title, highly detailed, creative and fun."

4

Drop The Track

Steps 4 & 5

How To:

  1. Open Google Gemini.
  2. Click the + icon.
  3. Select Generate Music.
  4. Paste prompt & generate.

Generate multiple times until you find a banger! 🔥

Band: The Rock Rebels Vibe: Fun College Life Beef: Slow WiFi
